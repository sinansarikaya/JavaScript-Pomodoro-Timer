What is Pomodoro Technique?

  • STEP 1: Pick a task
  • STEP 2: Set a 25-minute timer
  • STEP 3: Work on tour task full concentration and until the time is up
  • STEP 4: Take a 5 minute break
  • STEP 5: Every 4 pomodoros, take a longer 15 - 20 minute break
